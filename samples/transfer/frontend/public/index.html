Transfers - Information

This is a Demo to demonstrate Transfers. Transfers can be carried out via the account api only.
Go here to read more about transfers
When doing transfers, Please be aware that the money being transferred is the money available in your Rave account (availabe balance).
A successful transfer is queued to be processed. You can look up the status of a transfer by checking the transfers tab in your Rave dashboard.

Use the following account numbers below.
Acc No: 0690000031 | Bank: Access Bank
Acc No: 5900102340 | Bank: Providus Bank

In general, we highly recommend that you check out the codebase generated for you to see how transfers can be implemented.
Replace the public and secret keys in the .env file to your own rave sandbox public and secret keys.
